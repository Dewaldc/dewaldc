about

HoKuLi is a modern, luxurious, and earthy wellness lounge blending bistro coffee culture, holistic healing, curated whiskey, and premium lifestyle offerings — crafted for the modern professional.

who we are

HoKuLi brings together artisanal coffee, curated whiskey selections, holistic therapies, and cannabis‑infused edibles — all within a sophisticated, Instagram‑worthy environment. It is a space where ritual meets relaxation, luxury meets grounding, and community thrives.

mission

To create a sanctuary where wellness meets indulgence, fostering mindful living and meaningful connections through exceptional guest experiences.

vision

To become a leading lifestyle brand that redefines social and wellness spaces through a harmonious blend of luxury, sustainability, and sensory-driven design.

unique value proposition

Unlike traditional cafés or spas, HoKuLi unifies coffee culture, holistic healing, and premium spirits under one roof — offering an immersive, elevated, and modern lifestyle experience for young professionals.
